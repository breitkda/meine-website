Georgia

My trip to Georgia was a unique experience combining nature, culture and history. From the vibrant capital Tbilisi to the stunning mountains of Kazbegi, every place had its own character.

Highlights

  • Tbilisi old town and architecture
  • Kazbegi mountains and Gergeti Trinity Church
  • Local cuisine and wine culture

Impression

Georgia offers a mix of European and Asian influences. The hospitality of the people and the diversity of landscapes make it a truly special destination.
